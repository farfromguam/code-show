About

I am a graphic designer with the belief that all design should support rather than distract from the message. The pieces that I create are purposefully designed to solve specific problems. I believe that design should come from teamwork to achieve the highest quality product possible, rather than one person alone in front of a computer. I have a strong knowledge of the psychological aspects of visual communication and use this as a tool to solve problems. Working as a part of your team, I feel that I can become a valued asset to you and your company.
